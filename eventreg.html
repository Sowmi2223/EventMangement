Sizzle & Serve Showdown 🥘

This is a fast-paced cooking challenge where participants must prepare dishes that are sizzling hot and ready to serve.
The focus is on cooking techniques that involve grilling or searing — anything that results in sizzling, mouth-watering flavors.
Judges evaluate the presentation, creativity, and taste.

Cold Cuisine 🍦

This challenge focuses on creating gourmet dishes that don’t require any heat, ideal for showcasing chilled presentations and creative culinary ideas.
Think of fresh salads, sushi, and other cold dishes that rely on flavor combinations rather than cooking techniques.
The challenge emphasizes creativity and the judges assess the overall taste of cold dishes.

Grill & Thrill Challenge 🍗

In this challenge, participants focus on grilling techniques, taking their grilling skills to the next level.
It combines the art of cooking with fire and heat, and participants must not only grill food but also create thrilling experiences with unique grilling methods.
The goal is to produce dishes that excite both the taste buds and the senses with the dramatic flair of grilling.
